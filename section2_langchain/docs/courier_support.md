# QuickBite Courier Support

## Courier Earnings and Payouts
Couriers are paid per completed delivery: base fare + distance pay + 100% of customer tips. Payouts are transferred every Monday via direct deposit.

## Safety and Vehicle Requirements
Couriers may use bicycle, e-bike, scooter, or motor vehicle. All motorized vehicles must carry active liability insurance and valid vehicle registration.

## Incident Reporting
In case of accident or emergency during delivery, couriers must press the in-app Emergency Button to notify safety operations and dispatch local authorities immediately.
