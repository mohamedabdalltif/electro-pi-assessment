# QuickBite Delivery Terms

## Operating Hours and Service Areas
QuickBite operates 24/7 in metro areas. Delivery zones are divided into:
- **Core Zone**: within 5 km of the restaurant.
- **Extended Zone**: 5–15 km from the restaurant.

## Delivery Fees and Surge Pricing
- Base delivery fee is $2.99 for orders within 3 km.
- An additional $0.50 per km is charged beyond 3 km.
- During peak hours (12:00–14:00 and 19:00–21:00) or severe weather, surge pricing of up to 1.5x applies.

## Order Tracking and Communication
Real-time GPS tracking is available once a courier accepts the order. Direct chat and masked phone calling are enabled between courier and customer until 15 minutes after delivery.
