# QuickBite Refund Policy

## Eligibility for Refunds
Customers are eligible for a full or partial refund under the following conditions:
- **Missing Items**: If an item is missing from the delivery, a full refund for that item will be issued within 3–5 business days.
- **Incorrect Order**: If the restaurant delivers a completely wrong order, the customer can request a 100% refund or a free replacement.
- **Excessive Delay**: If a delivery is delayed by more than 45 minutes beyond the ETA due to courier error, customers receive a 50% delivery fee credit.

## Refund Request Window
All refund requests must be submitted through the app within 24 hours of delivery. Requests after 24 hours are reviewed case-by-case.

## Non-Refundable Scenarios
Refunds will NOT be issued if:
- The customer provided an incorrect delivery address.
- The courier waited 10 minutes with no response from the customer.
